# Tools Used

This project combines multiple AI and media tools.

---

## YouTube Downloader

Used to download the original news broadcast.

---

## UVR (Ultimate Vocal Remover)

Used to extract presenter voice from background audio.

---

## Audacity

Used to clean and prepare the audio dataset.

---

## ElevenLabs

Used for voice cloning and narration generation.

---

## Leonardo AI

Used to enhance and upscale presenter images.

---

## HeyGen

Used to animate the presenter and create lip synchronization.

---

## Lightworks

Used for final video editing and news broadcast assembly.
